    FAQs

    How do I create an account?
    Create an account by clicking the "Sign Up" button on the homepage. You can register using your email or Google account.

    How can I search for a doctor?
    Use the search bar on the homepage to look for doctors by symptom, location, or specialty.

    How do I book an appointment?
    Select a doctor from the search results, choose an available time slot, and confirm your appointment.

    How do I register as a doctor?
    Click on "Register as a Doctor" on the homepage. Provide your medical credentials and complete the verification process.

    What credentials are required?
    You need a valid medical license and proof of identity to register as an authorized doctor.
